vactoair

Syntax: vactoair <wavenumber> [P] [T] [P(water)]

where

  • P: the air pressure in Torr inside the spectrometer tank.
  • T: the air temperature in degrees Celsius inside the spectrometer tank.
  • P(water): the partial pressure of water in Torr inside the spectrometer tank.
For a given vacuum - wavenumber the air wavelength is computed. Pressure and temperature of the air can be specified optionally. An air pressure of 760 Torr and a temperature of 20 deg C are assumed by default. The air wavelength and the refractive index of air are printed out. Uses the forumulae of Birch and Downs for the refractive index of air.

Example:

vactoair 30000. 760. 24.

calculates the air wavelength corresponding to 30000.0/cm at a pressure of 760 Torr and 24 degrees C. No water vapor.
