fourtran

Syntax: fourtran

Generate a FFT of the points in the r array. The transform is left in the r array as complex pairs. The amplitude is left in the lower half of tr, destroying the original content; the argument (phase) is in the upper half. This is used for relatively short transforms and for transforms of spectra. To transform the raw interferogram, use the transform command, which also applies the phase correction.

Example:

	sinc 20. 1024      (must be power of 2 points in 'r')
	plot               (display contents of 'r')
	fourtran           (calculate FFT)
	plot	           (display transformed function)
