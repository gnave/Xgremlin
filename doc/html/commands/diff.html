diff

Syntax: diff [< ibeg > < iend > ]

subtracts points between ibeg and iend of tr from corresponding points in r and leaves the result in r. If no parameters are given all of tr is subtracted from r.

Examples:

diff
subtracts all of tr from the r array

diff 1 100
subtracts points 1 to 100 of the tr array from the corresponding points in the r array. The resulting points are left in the r array
