stretch

Syntax:

stretch <ns>

Stretch data in the 'r' array by a factor of ns (default = 2) by Fourier interpolation. i.e. take the Fourier transform of the r array, padd the data to the next power of 2 with zeros, and then take the inverse transform

This is used to interpolate extra points into the line profile.
