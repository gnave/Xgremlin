shift

Syntax:

shift <istep> shift <step>
  • <istep> - an integral point number shift for the 'r' array.
  • <step> - a non-integral shift for the 'r' array.
The integral shift merely moves the points about circularly in the 'r' array. The non-integral shift uses a forward FFT and an inverse FFT to shift the points a non-integral step - 'tr' is destroyed.
