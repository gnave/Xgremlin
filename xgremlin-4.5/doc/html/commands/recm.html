recm

Syntax:

recm [<r> <b> ]

recall the positions of either red (r), blue (b) or all (default) graphics markers.

Examples: 

 
recm b
will recall the positions of all stored blue markers and will re-draw them in the plot 
recm
will recall all stored markers
