photonscm-1

Syntax:

photonscm-1 [<nm>]

Convert data points in the 'r' array from either W/cm-1 or W/nm to photons/cm-1. RELATIVE SCALE ONLY.

This command may be used in radiometric applications to transform the radiance of a standard source into a form that may be compared to data measured with e.g. photomulitpliers on a FTS. Radiances of standard sources are commonly given in W / cm^2 nm sr. A photomultiplier attached to a FTS measures photons/cm-1.

Examples:

photons/cm-1     # converts W/cm-1  -->  photons/cm-1
photons/cm-1 nm  # converts W/nm    -->  photons/cm-1
